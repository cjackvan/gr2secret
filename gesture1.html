GESTURE 1

Gesture 1 is an analog DJ mixer and the inaugural effort from Garcia Register, a collaboration between Andrew Kilpatrick and Charlie Van. As a result of their distinct yet complementary practices, Gesture 1 draws from the rich history of audio technology in dance music culture while maintaining a contemporary approach. Each Gesture 1 is handmade and tested by Charlie and Andrew in Montreal QC and Vancouver BC.

Solid-state architecture and choice components throughout Gesture 1 ensure an optimally transparent listening experience, revealing the slightest nuance in source material and playback equipment. Switchable crossover points on the channel isolators offer playability without compromising its minimalist character. A contrast of classic and modern interfacing components invite a broad range of use, free from association with any one style, genre, or era. Gesture 1 reflects Garcia Register’s primary motivation in design — a deep appreciation for recorded music in all its forms.

Frequency Response: 20Hz - 20kHz

RIAA Conformity: +-0.1dB

THD: Less than 0.0003%

SNR: 95dB below unity gain
